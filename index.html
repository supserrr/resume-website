About

I’m a student and aspiring software engineer based in Rwanda, currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering at African Leadership University (ALU). My academic journey has equipped me with a strong foundation in programming, problem-solving, and innovative thinking, while also fostering my passion for designing and creating.

While studying software engineering, I’ve developed a keen interest in combining technical skills with creativity. As a graphic designer, I enjoy crafting visually appealing designs that communicate ideas effectively. My expertise extends to web design, where I build user-friendly and responsive websites using modern tools and frameworks. Additionally, I’m passionate about content creation and cinematography, allowing me to tell compelling stories through multimedia platforms.

On the technical side, I’m proficient in various programming languages like Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and C++.

My love for technology and creativity drives me to explore new challenges and opportunities. Inspired by projects like freeCodeCamp's Full Stack Web Development Certification (though I haven’t completed it yet), I aim to deepen my knowledge in areas such as database management, front-end and back-end development, and automation. This multidisciplinary approach allows me to tackle problems from multiple angles, ensuring efficient and innovative solutions.

Currently, I’m open to freelance opportunities or internships that allow me to apply my skills in real-world scenarios while continuing to grow as a developer and designer. If you’d like to learn more about my work, feel free to reach out—I’d be happy to share my portfolio or discuss potential collaborations.
